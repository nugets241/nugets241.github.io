About

I am a passionate software engineering student dedicated to crafting intuitive and practical applications. My strongest skills lie in web and mobile app development, as well as embedded systems development, where I utilize technologies like React, React Native, JavaScript, Java, C++, and Figma. I believe in designing software with the user in mind, seamlessly blending thoughtful design with efficient engineering.


Problem-solving and debugging are at the core of my work. I approach every projects as if I were the end user, focusing on clear and straightforward solutions. While I value simplicity, I am also flexible in adapting to client requirements, offering suggestions when appropriate to achieve the best possible outcomes.


My ultimate goal is to become a versatile software engineer capable of excelling in various roles, whether in front-end, back-end, UI/UX, embedded systems, or AI/ML. Currently, I am refreshing my memory by taking the Responsive Web Design course on freeCodeCamp.


In addition to coding, I enjoy exploring how things work and building my own solutions. My curiosity and practical mindset recently led me to build an electric bike, combining cost efficiency with technical creativity. When I'm not working on software projects, I play guitar, engage in strategy games like chess and Dota 2, or brainstorm my next engineering challenge.
