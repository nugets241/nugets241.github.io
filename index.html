About

I'm a passionate software engineering student with a broad interest in all areas of software development, from frontend and backend to embedded systems and AI/ML. While I'm eager to expand across the field, most of my hands-on experience so far has been in frontend development, where I've had the most opportunities to grow.


I've worked on real-world projects using technologies like React, TypeScript, and Next.js, and I'm currently a software developer trainee at M-Files, where I focus on building accessible, reusable UI components in collaboration with designers and engineers. Previously, I interned at Hiava Oy as a full-stack developer on a logistics data management application.


Outside of work, I continuously level up my skills through courses, books, and personal projects, such as designing and building an electric bike from scratch. My earlier career in healthcare helped me develop soft skills that are just as vital in tech, such as teamwork, problem-solving, and staying calm under pressure.


I'm always looking to take on meaningful challenges and become a well-rounded software engineer, wherever that journey leads.
